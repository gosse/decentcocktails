+++
title: Margarita
+++

notes: A margarita done the right way.

ingredients: 
  - (1 pinch) salt (kosher)
  - (1 1/2 oz.) tequila 
  - (1 oz.) cointreau 
  - (1/2 oz.) lime juice, freshly squeezed
  
instructions: 
  - Add all ingredients to shaker with ice, shake. 
  - Strain into chilled glass
  - Garnish with lime wedge.
