### Ingredients
  - 2 oz rum (traditionally dark rum)
  - 4-5 oz cola
  - 1/2 lime
  
### Instructions
  - Fill a highball glass with ice cubes
  - Squeeze the juice from half a lime into the glass
  - Drop the lime shell into the glass 
  - Pour in the rum
  - Top with cola
  - Gently stir to combine
