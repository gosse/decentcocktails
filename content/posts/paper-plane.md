### Ingredients
  - 3/4 ounce bourbon
  - 3/4 ounce Aperol
  - 3/4 ounce Amaro Nonino Quintessentia
  - 3/4 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed

  
### Instructions
  - Shake with ice
  - Strain into a (chillded) coupe glass
