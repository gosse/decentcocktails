### Ingredients
  - 1.5 oz rum (e.g., Smith & Cross)
  - .75 oz Campari 
  - 1.5 oz pineapple juice 
  - .5 oz lime juice 
  - .5 oz demerara syrup

### Instructions
  - Shake all ingredients with ice 
  - Double strain into a rocks glass. 
  - Garnish with pineapple leaves and a pineapple wedge
