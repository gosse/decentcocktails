### Ingredients
  - 2 oz rum 
  - 4 oz pineapple juice (fresh if possible)
  - 1 oz orange juice (fresh if possible)
  - 1 oz Coconut Cream
  
### Instructions
  - Shake with whole and crushed ice
  - Strain into a hurricane glass filled with ice
  - Top with grated nutmeg
