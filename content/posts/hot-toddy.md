*notes: Great for a sore throat, cold, or cold evening.*

### Ingredients 
  - (1 oz.) Bourbon
  - (1/2 oz.) Honey
  - (1 oz.) Lemon Juice, freshly squeezed  
  - (4-8 oz.) Hot water
  
### Instructions 
  - Add bourbon, honey, and lemon juice to hot water, stir until honey dissolves. 
  - Garnish with a lemon peel.
