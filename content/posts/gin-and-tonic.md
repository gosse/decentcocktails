*notes: A classic G&T*

### Ingredients 
  - (2 oz.) gin (Jack Pine, Tanqueray, or whatever you have) 
  - (3-6 oz.) tonic water (depending on how strong you want it)
  - lime wedges,
  
### Instructions 
  - Fill glass with ice. 
  - Add in gin, squeeze juice of lime into glass. 
  - Add tonic, stir
  - garnish with remaining lime wedge.
