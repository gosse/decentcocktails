*notes: A classic Cuban cocktail*

### Ingredients
  - (1 oz.) honey 
  - (1 oz.) lime juice, freshly squeezed 
  - (2 oz.) rum
  - (2 oz.) club soda
  - lime wedge

### Instructions 
  - Shake honey, lime juice, rum with ice. 
  - Strain into clay mug with fresh ice, top with club soda. 
  - Garnish with a lime wedge.
